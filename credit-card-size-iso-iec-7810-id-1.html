Credit card size (ISO/IEC 7810 ID-1)

By Sebastian Corsini — real-time information, with years of fact-checking experience.

ID-1 credit card size is 85.60 × 53.98 mm (3.370 × 2.125 in), typically with ~3.18 mm (~0.125 in) corner radius. Values follow ISO/IEC 7810.
