FAQ

What tire pressure should I use?

Follow the vehicle's door placard. Many cars specify around 30–35 PSI (207–241 kPa) cold.

Is the correct pressure on the tire sidewall?

No, use the vehicle placard. Sidewall shows max rating, not the vehicle spec.
