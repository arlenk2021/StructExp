A4 paper size (ISO 216)

By Sebastian Corsini — real-time information, with years of fact-checking experience.

A4 paper measures 210 × 297 mm (8.27 × 11.69 in). The aspect ratio is 1:√2 (≈1.414), defined by ISO 216.
